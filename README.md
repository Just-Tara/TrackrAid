# TrackrAid: Your Personal Expense Tracker

## Overview

This project helps you manage your personal finances effortlessly. It lets you easily track your income and expenses, providing clear insights into your spending habits so you can make smarter financial decisions.

## Description

Managing your money can feel like a chore, but it doesn't have to be. TrackrAid is a modern, intuitive expense tracker designed to simplify your financial life. It helps you effortlessly record daily transactions, visualize your spending patterns, and stay on top of your budget, all within a clean and responsive interface. Whether you're tracking small daily coffees or major monthly bills, TrackrAid gives you the clarity you need to make smarter financial decisions.

## Usage

Getting started with TrackrAid is straightforward. Here’s how you can use the application:

1.  **Sign Up or Log In:**
    *   When you first open the application, you'll land on the login page.
    *   You can create a new account by clicking the "Sign Up" link, providing a username, email, and password.
    *   Alternatively, you can log in with your existing credentials or use the "Continue with Google" option for a quick sign-in.

2.  **Onboarding (First-Time Users):**
    *   If it's your first time, you'll see a brief onboarding flow explaining the app's key features. You can click through these slides or "Skip" to go directly to the dashboard.

3.  **Dashboard Overview:**
    *   Once logged in, you'll see your main dashboard. This page provides a quick summary of your financial status:
        *   Your current balance.
        *   Total income.
        *   Total expenses.
        *   A "Balance Circle" indicating the percentage of income saved.
        *   A "Recent Transactions" section showing your latest three activities.
    *   On larger screens, a sidebar on the right will display "Income vs Expense" pie charts and a "Weekly Spending" bar chart for visual insights.

4.  **Adding a New Transaction:**
    *   To add a transaction, click the large `+` button, typically found at the bottom right of the screen on mobile or within the desktop view.
    *   On the "Add Transaction" page, select whether it's an "Income" or "Expense."
    *   Fill in the `Title`, `Amount`, and choose a `Category`.
    *   You can also add an optional `Note` for more details.
    *   The current date and time are pre-filled but represent the transaction timestamp.
    *   Click "Save" to add the transaction to your records.

5.  **Viewing and Managing Transactions:**
    *   Navigate to the "Transactions" page using the navigation bar (bottom on mobile, left on desktop).
    *   Here you'll see a comprehensive list of all your recorded transactions.
    *   You can use the search bar to find transactions by name, date, or amount.
    *   Filter transactions by "All," "Income," or "Expense" using the tabs provided.
    *   To view detailed information about a specific transaction, simply click on it. This will take you to a "Full Transaction" page.
    *   To delete a transaction, click the trash icon next to it in the list. A confirmation modal will appear before deletion.

6.  **Settings:**
    *   Access the "Settings" page from the navigation bar.
    *   **User Profile:** View your name, email, and initials.
    *   **Appearance:** Toggle between "Light Mode" and "Dark Mode" to change the application's theme.
    *   **Currency:** (Placeholder functionality) Select your preferred display currency.
    *   **Data Management:**
        *   **Reset All Data:** Permanently delete all your transactions after confirmation. Use with caution!
        *   **Export Data:** Generate and download a PDF report of all your transactions.
    *   **Log Out:** Securely sign out of your account.

## Features

*   **Intuitive Transaction Tracking**: Easily add income and expense entries with details like title, amount, category, date, and notes.
*   **Comprehensive Dashboard**: Get an instant overview of your current balance, total income, and total expenses.
*   **Visual Spending Insights**: Understand your financial health at a glance with interactive pie charts (income vs. expense) and bar charts (weekly spending).
*   **Efficient Transaction Management**: View all your transactions in one place, with robust filtering by type and a search function for quick lookups.
*   **Secure User Authentication**: Sign up and log in securely using email and password or streamline the process with Google OAuth.
*   **Personalized Experience**: Switch between light and dark themes to suit your preference.
*   **Data Export & Management**: Generate and download a PDF report of your transactions, or easily clear all your data if you need a fresh start.
*   **Responsive Design**: A seamless experience across desktop and mobile devices.
